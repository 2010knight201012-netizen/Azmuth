    About WordSprint

    WordSprint is a competitive typing race game. Type words quickly to move your car toward the finish line. The faster and more accurately you type, the higher your score and ranking on the leaderboard.

    Features include WPM tracking, combo multipliers, racing animation, and a competitive leaderboard.
